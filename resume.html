What is Resume?


A resume is a written compilation of your education, work experience, credentials, and accomplishments. Most professional positions require applicants to submit a resume and cover letter as part of the application process.

HOW TO MAKE A RESUME FOR FIRST JOB:

  • Pick the right resume layout.
  • Make a meaningful education section.
  • Focus on your relevant experience.
  • Sprinkle the entire resume with key skills.
  • Include additional sections to boost chances
  • Compose a powerful introductory paragraph.
